Our Philosophy

A story of quiet
craftsmanship

MAISON was born from a simple belief: that true luxury lives in the details unseen. In the weight of a hem. The silence of a well-cut shoulder. The way fabric falls when no one is watching.

We source only the finest natural fibres — cashmere from the Mongolian highlands, silk from artisan looms in Lyon, linen from the fields of Normandy. Each piece is cut by hand.

How to Place an Order

  1. Select your size using the size guide above.
  2. Click "Order Now" to fill in your delivery details.
  3. Enter your name, email, phone number and delivery address.
  4. Add any special notes (gift wrap, colour preference, etc.)
  5. Submit — we'll confirm within 24 hours via email.
  6. Payment is arranged on delivery or via bank transfer.

All MAISON pieces are crafted from natural fibres and require careful handling. We recommend dry cleaning for structured pieces and hand washing in cold water for knits and delicate fabrics. Lay flat to dry. Iron on low heat with a pressing cloth. Store in a breathable garment bag away from direct sunlight to preserve colour and structure.

Standard Shipping (5–10 days): Free on all orders over $300.

Express Shipping (2–4 days): $25 flat rate worldwide.

Bangladesh Delivery: 1–3 business days via Pathao or Steadfast. Cash on delivery available within Dhaka.

Returns accepted within 14 days of receipt — items must be unworn with tags attached.
